Parkinator Help

Remembering where you parked

Parkinator lets you take a picture to remember where you parked. When you do, it also automatically gets the current location from your phone's GPS and places a pin on a map beneath the picture. To do this:

  1. Tap the information button (the i) on the bottom right of the homescreen
  2. Tap the camera icon on the next screen
  3. Take a picture
  4. Tap the Done button

Tweeting that your spot is open

Tap the action button (2nd button from the left). Tweeting your spot will only work if you have entered a valid Twitter username and password into the iOS Twitter settings.

Viewing available spots

Tap the seach button (2nd button from the right). Free spaces near your location will appear as pins on the map.
